Swedish Massage<

Swedish massage is basically a framework for all other types of Western massage practices and techniques. This includes sports massage, deep tissue massage, and aromatherapy massage...

Deep Tissue<

Deep tissue massage is a therapeutical massage technique which focuses on realigning deeper layers of muscles in the human body. Revitalizing energy and improved mood are generally the end effects of this type of body massage ...

Hot Stones Therapy<

Hot stone massage was designed as a special kind of revitalizing technique. This type of massage sees the therapist using stick and pre-heated stones as an extension of their own hands.
